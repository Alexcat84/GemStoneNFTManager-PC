Returns

Hassle-free returns for your GemSpots planters

Return Policy

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 days from the delivery date to return your GemSpots planter for a full refund.

Original Packaging

Items must be returned in their original packaging with all accessories and documentation.

Unused Condition

Planters must be in unused, original condition with no signs of wear or damage.

Proof of Purchase

Please include your order confirmation or receipt with your return.

How to Return

1

Contact Support

Email us at support@gemspots.com with your order number and reason for return.

2

Get Return Label

We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.

3

Package & Ship

Pack your item securely and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

4

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Information

Refund Methods

  • Original payment method (credit card, PayPal)
  • Credit card refunds
  • Store credit for future purchases

Processing Times

  • Credit Card: 5-7 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Credit card: 1-3 business days
  • Store Credit: Immediate

Shipping Costs

  • Free return shipping for defective items
  • Free return shipping for size exchanges
  • Customer pays return shipping for other reasons
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable

Non-Returnable Items

Please Note

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

Items used for planting or gardening
Items with signs of wear or damage
Items returned after 30 days
Items without original packaging
Custom or personalized items
Items acquired during special promotions
