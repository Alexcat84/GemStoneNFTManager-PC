Complete NFT Setup Guide

Everything you need to know to set up your NFT wallet and start collecting unique GemSpots crystal planters

Getting Started with NFTs

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are unique digital certificates that prove ownership of your GemSpots crystal planters. Each pot comes with its own NFT stored on the blockchain, making it verifiable and authentic.

Important

This guide will walk you through setting up a wallet, connecting to the blockchain network, and managing your NFT collection. Follow each step carefully for the best experience.

Installation Steps

Follow these steps to install and set up your wallet:

Download and Install

Visit the official website of your chosen wallet and download the browser extension or mobile app. Always download from official sources to avoid scams.

Create Your Wallet

Open the wallet and click "Create New Wallet". You'll be asked to create a strong password. Make sure to write it down in a safe place.

Backup Your Seed Phrase

Your wallet will generate a 12 or 24-word seed phrase. Write these words down in the exact order shown. This is your backup - never share it with anyone!

Verify Your Backup

The wallet will ask you to verify your seed phrase by selecting the words in the correct order. This ensures you have the backup correctly written down.

Configure the Blockchain Network

GemSpots NFTs are stored on the Polygon network. You'll need to add this network to your wallet:

Polygon Network Details

Network Name: Polygon Mainnet

RPC URL: https://polygon-rpc.com

Chain ID: 137

Currency Symbol: POL

Block Explorer: https://polygonscan.com

Add Polygon Network to Your Wallet

For detailed instructions on how to add Polygon network to your specific wallet, visit the official documentation:

MetaMask Official Guide Trust Wallet Official Guide Coinbase Wallet Official Guide SafePal Official Guide

Get POL Tokens

You'll need POL tokens to pay for transaction fees. You can buy POL on exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, or other cryptocurrency exchanges.

Viewing Your GemSpots NFTs

Once you have your wallet set up, you can view and manage your GemSpots NFTs:

Connect Your Wallet

When you purchase a GemSpots planter, you'll receive an NFT certificate. Connect your wallet to our website to view your collection.

View NFT Details

Click on your NFT to see detailed information including the crystal type, rarity, dimensions, and ownership history.

Verify Authenticity

Use the blockchain explorer to verify that your NFT is authentic and see its complete transaction history.

Security Best Practices

Keep your NFTs and wallet secure with these important tips:

Security Checklist

  • Never share your seed phrase with anyone
  • Use a hardware wallet for large amounts
  • Always verify website URLs before connecting
  • Keep your wallet software updated
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

Can't See My NFT

Make sure you're connected to the Polygon network and refresh your wallet. NFTs can take a few minutes to appear after purchase.

Transaction Failed

Check that you have enough POL tokens for gas fees. Try increasing the gas limit or try again during less busy network times.

Wrong Network

If you're on the wrong network, switch to Polygon Mainnet in your wallet settings. Your NFTs won't be visible on other networks.
