Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GemSpots products and services

General Questions

What is GemSpots?

GemSpots is a premium collection of crystal planters that combine the beauty of natural crystals with modern design. Each planter comes with a unique NFT certificate that proves authenticity and ownership.

What makes GemSpots unique?

Each GemSpots planter is handcrafted with genuine crystals and comes with a unique NFT certificate stored on the Polygon blockchain. This ensures authenticity, provenance, and creates a digital collectible that can be traded or displayed.

Are the crystals real?

Yes, all crystals used in GemSpots planters are 100% genuine and ethically sourced. Each crystal is carefully selected for its quality and energy properties.

NFT & Blockchain

What is the NFT certificate?

The NFT certificate is a digital certificate of authenticity stored on the Polygon blockchain. It contains information about your specific planter, including its unique characteristics, crystal type, and manufacturing details.

Do I need cryptocurrency knowledge to own a GemSpots planter?

No! We provide a complete guide to help you set up your wallet and receive your NFT certificate. Our support team is also available to assist you through the process.

Can I sell or trade my NFT certificate?

Yes, your NFT certificate is a digital asset that you can sell, trade, or transfer to others. However, the physical planter and NFT certificate should ideally stay together to maintain their value.

What blockchain is used for the NFTs?

We use the Polygon blockchain, which offers low transaction fees and fast processing times. This makes it easy and affordable to manage your NFT certificate.

Products & Care

How do I care for my crystal planter?

Clean your planter with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals. For the crystal, you can cleanse it with moonlight, sage, or by placing it in salt water (check crystal-specific care instructions).

What plants are suitable for these planters?

Most small to medium plants work well. We recommend succulents, herbs, or small flowering plants. Ensure proper drainage and don't overwater.

Are the planters waterproof?

Yes, all our planters are designed to be waterproof and include proper drainage systems to keep your plants healthy.

Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and POL (Polygon).

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within Canada and 7-14 business days internationally. Express shipping options are available.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can track your package in real-time.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any questions about GemSpots products or NFTs.
